How to register

Users of Sapphire Yours can register online using a registration form at our contract partner using credit card information. For other ways of registering (especially in Europe) please check the conditions of our partner.



After the registration process is finished you will get an e-mail containing your registration code for the programm. Just enter this code at the startup dialog box of Sapphire Yours to turn your trial version into an unlimited full version.
Your registering key will of course also work for all future releases of the game.

Why registering?
