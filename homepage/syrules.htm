Sapphire Yours - Rules

The game is organized in levels each of which is a puzzle of its own and has to be solved independently from all others. Indeed, you can start playing whatever level you like.

Luckily the rules of the game are quite simple:
  1. The computer tells you how many emeralds and sapphires you have to collect for the level.
  2. You collect this many.
  3. You try to escape through the exit.
Unluckily, altough the rules are simple, the implications of the rules are not. You can not just go ahead and grab all the emerald on your way without thinking a little forward.

To demonstrate this, we have here a screenshot of one of the easier levels:

The brown stuff that fills most of the area is just earth that can be dug away by you (the little miner at the left side). The emeralds are of course the green crystals and this little door with the blue border is the exit. The exit is closed at the moment, but it will be opened as soon as you have collected enough emeralds. The grey boulders that are mixed up with the emeralds are not for collecting but are merrily there to make your live a little harder.
As you immediately notice, there are some emeralds that are totaly blocked by boulders and absolutely unreachable at first. But luckily there is a kind of gravity in the game that lets everything fall down to the bottom (except of you, the miner). So as soon, as you dig away some earth beneath the boulders and emeralds everything will roll and move down and eventually you can collect all emeralds (if you are clever enough to do everything in the right way - but this is the puzzle, isn't it?).

To make this whole process more clear, we give you another picture, where the player has already collected some emeralds:

After you managed to collect all the gems you need, the exit will open. Just walk through the open door and the level is finally solved.

Of course there is much more to say about the behaviour of the game elements, but you should just start the game and find out these things yourself.
